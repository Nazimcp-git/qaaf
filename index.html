The Vision

Art Rooted in Revelation.

﷽

QAF — Quran Art Festival is a celebration of the sacred and the beautiful. It is a space where artists, reciters, poets, and designers gather under one vision: to express the timeless words of the Quran through the lens of art.

Born from the belief that creativity is an act of gratitude, QAF invites participants to channel their God-given talents in service of something greater than themselves. Every brushstroke, every note, every verse spoken is a form of remembrance.

Open to all age groups and skill levels, QAF is not merely a competition — it is a movement. A gathering of souls who believe that art and faith are not opposites, but mirrors of the same light.

"And We have certainly made the Quran easy for remembrance."

— Quran 54:17

Disciplines

Seven disciplines, one language.

Each program is a gateway — an invitation to express the Quran through a distinct artistic form.

01

Ulul Albab

Comprehensive evaluation in Qira'at, Hifz, Tafseer, and Tajweed.

02

Wama Adraka

Presenting Quranic lessons to the general public in accessible language.

03

Ahsanul Qasas

Visual representation of historical stories from the Holy Quran.

04

Fastabikul Khairath

Talent quiz based on foundational texts on the sciences of the Quran.

05

Qawlan Sadeeda

Responding to modern criticisms and allegations against the Quran.

06

Hathoo Burhanakum

Group debate competition centered on the Science of the Quran.

07

Ballig Ma Nussila Ilaikka

Writing commentary mimicking the style of ancient, traditional Tafsirs.
